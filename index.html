About Me

Jaylee Maruk is an LA based Union Actress, singer/songwriter, and dancer. She also has studied Martial Arts including: Kendo, Kung Fu, and sword fighting. Jaylee is a dual citzen with Canada and the United States. She enjoys traveling, cooking, trying new restaurants, eating, and computer coding. Jaylee's favorite foods are: Steak, Chinese food, Sushi, Spaghetti, Pho, KFC, McDonald's, and Taco Bell. Some would say she's an eclectic eater, but she feels she has a highbrow and lowbrow taste to be a well rounded eater.
